🎬 Set up your Content Calendar

This calendar syncs in real-time with your whole team. You just need a free Firebase project — it takes about 3 minutes to set up.

Step 1: Go to console.firebase.google.com and click Add project. Give it any name (e.g. "my-yt-calendar") and click through the setup.
Step 2: In your project sidebar, click Build → Realtime Database. Click Create database, choose any region, and start in test mode.
Step 3: Go to Project Settings (gear icon) → General → scroll to Your apps → click the </> web icon. Register the app (any nickname). Copy the firebaseConfig values below.
